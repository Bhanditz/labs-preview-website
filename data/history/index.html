  • Black-and-white ethnographic photographs from the Swedish Museum of Ethnography

    More than 1,700 black-and-white ethnographic photographs taken in various continents, from the collection of the Swedish Museum of Ethnography in Stockholm.

    Dataset provided by Etnografiska museet. Contact carlotta@etnografiska.se for more details.

  • Newsreels from Sound and Vision on the Netherlands in the 20th century

    More than 3,000 videos from the Open Images (Open Beelden) dataset of the Netherlands Institute for Sound and Vision. The collection contains newsreels from the Polygoon collection and several other films on the Netherlands in the twentieth century. It contains historical news events, with topics such as art, culture, transportation, health and healthcare, nature, politics, sports, pets and fashion.

    Dataset provided by Netherlands Institute for Sound and Vision. Contact hvbiessum@beeldengeluid.nl for more details.


    View this data in:
    Portal API Console
    links History

  • Maps and Drawings from the Catálogo Colectivo De La Red De Bibliotecas De Los Archivos Estatales

    More than 7,000 maps, city plans & architectural drawings/plans from the Catálogo Colectivo de la Red de Bibliotecas de los Archivos Estatales. The collection is a union catalog of state archives and libraries in Spain. Spanish language. This dataset also contains ancient maps from Barcelona, please add 'where: barcelona' as keyword to narrow down the search.

    Dataset provided by Catálogo Colectivo de la Red de Bibliotecas de los Archivos Estatales. Contact ccbae.dglab@mcu.es for more details.
